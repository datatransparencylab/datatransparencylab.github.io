Data Transparency Lab + Open Data Institute

Data has the potential to provide valuable insight to improve our lives as individuals and our societies. The Open Data Institute (ODI) is catalysing the evolution of open data culture to create economic, environmental, and social value. The ODI believes that there is huge value to be derived from the ethical usage of aggregate personal data, but that personal data is never open by default.

A pillar of the Data Transparency Lab (DTL) is the preservation of people's trust in how their data is used online. DTL aims to generate knowledge and tools to improve transparency around how personal data is accessed and used by others, and establish best practices for sharing of personal data. The ODI joined the DTL community because it believes that the power of open data is closely linked to the preservation of this trust.

By joining the DTL community, the ODI seeks to explore how open data can contribute to increased transparency and awareness of how personal data is being accessed and used.
