Announcing DTL-supported tools: AirCloak and FloodWatch

26 Nov 2015

The Data Transparency Lab is now collaborating with the creators of: AirCloak, cutting-edge data anonymization technology and FloodWatch, a Chrome extension that tracks the ads you see as you browse the internet and presents them and info about them visually.

We support these innovative technologies to help build a more transparent web.
