Some Datasets

Floodwatch

Floodwatch want to assemble the largest amount of advertising data. It includes a list with all the advetisement a set of users have seen while browsing together with some categorisation. The ultimate target is sharing thata data with academic institutions. A first snapshot of the Floodwatch Dataset has been published via Aircloak.

Facebook CPM and CPC per Audience
This dataset has been kindly contributed by one of 2015 grantees, the Carlos III University. It contains Cost Per Click (CPC) and Cost Per Thousand Impressions (CPM) value for multiple audiences (resulting of the combinations of up to 10 parameters) in 60 countries. The dataset contains the daily evolution of CPC and CPM during one year and it's available at the DTL Datasharing Website.
